Welcome to part I-lost-count of Tofu's identity crisis. Today we will ponder, why is Tofu Tofu?

Something I noticed about me is that, I don't like describing myself as "I am something," and I prefer "I like doing something" instead. I am not an artist, but I like drawing. I am not a programmer, but I like coding. Maybe I just hate labeling myself. I am Tofu and I am nothing else. And Tofu likes doing things sometimes.
