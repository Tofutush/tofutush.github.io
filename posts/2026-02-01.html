January Recap

Here comes the first installment of me disciplining myself into writing monthly recaps. I need a template for this thing.

How was the month?

Cold. Snow everywhere. For the first part, winter break wasn't over, and I was honestly quite bored. And then I got back to school and reunited with more friends. And then it started to snow a ton and that got classes all canceled.

When I got back to the dorm, I unpacked, and swept the entire place. It was not big but I still got so tired that my stomach started growling like crazy.

Milestones

  • I spent a whole year being eligible for the death penalty, yet not being sentenced to death.
  • I started the second semester of college, despite not having any classes due to the snow.

Stuff I did

  • Wrote a couple chapters of Spy School.
  • Made a writing system.
  • Wrote a quiz framework and published quizzes.
  • Made a tilde.town page.
  • Made a microblog.
  • Started the Tadlet Kingdom Adventures project.

Stuff I want to do

  • Continue doing the Tadlet Kingdom Adventures project.
  • Do well at school.
  • Reread the Wings of Fire books to prepare for book 16.
