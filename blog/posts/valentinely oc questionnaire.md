1. What name do you go by online?

2. How long have you been creating OCs?

3. Where do you keep all the info on your OCs? In a notebook? On a website? Do you share your OC profiles or stories with anyone?

4. Do you consider yourself primarily a writer, artist, role-player, or something else?

5. What first inspired you to start making characters?

6. What piece of media or fictional world has influenced your OCs the most?

7. Which creator, professional or not, inspires you most?

8. How many OCs do you estimate you have?

9. What genre do you enjoy creating in most? What genre do you rarely create in?

10. What is your favorite character archetype?

11. What character trope are you tired of seeing?

12. What trope do you avoid?

13. What trope or archetype do you not have and want to create?

14. What shipping trope can you never resist? What shipping trope is not your cup of tea?

15. What emotion do you enjoy exploring most?

16. What emotion is hardest for you to write?

17. What is your opinion on dark themes and characters?

18. What design aesthetic best describes your tastes? What design aesthetic do you dislike?

20. What charm point/moe point do you often include in your OC designs?

21. Explain your OC process: do you design characters visually before writing them? Do your characters tend to change significantly over time?

22. How often do you redesign OCs?

23. Do you keep character references organized? What tools, apps, or websites do you utilize to help keep you organized?

24. Do you listen to music while creating?

25. Link a song or album you associate with your OC/story.

26. What is one thing all your characters seem to have in common?

27. What is something you hope people feel when they see your work?

28. What kind of people do you think are drawn to your characters and stories?

29. What keeps you creating OCs?

## PART II: YOUR OC COLLECTION

1. What was your very first OC like?

2. Do you still have your oldest OC?

3. Which OC was created most recently? What inspired them?

4. Which OC are you most proud of?

5. Which OC was easiest to create?

6. Which OC was hardest to create?

7. From you or from others, which OC receives the most attention?

8. From you or from others, which OC do you wish received more attention?

9. Which OC is your favorite to draw? Which is the hardest to draw?

10. Which OC is your favorite to write? Which is the hardest to write?

11. Which OC would you trust with your deepest secret?

12. Which OC absolutely cannot be trusted?

13. What OC is most similar to you, in appearance, personality, or both?

14. Which OC was born from anger?

15. Which OC was born from sadness?

16. Which OC exists because there was something you wanted to see in fiction but could not find?

17. Choose an OC. What fate are they doomed to repeat?

18. If all your OCs met in one room, what would happen?

## PART III: YOUR FAVORITE OC

1. What is your favorite OC's full name? Do they have nicknames?

2. Describe their appearance. What is their voice like?

3. What is their occupation?

4. What is their most distinctive feature?

5. What first impression do people get from them?

6. What is their greatest flaw?

7. What is something they secretly wish people understood about them?

8. How would their best friend describe them?

9. What event shaped them most?

10. What secret are they keeping?

11. Who is most important to them?

12. What qualities attract them to someone? What qualities immediately repel them?

13. What would break their heart?

14. What is a skill they're unexpectedly good at?

15. What is something they're terrible at?

16. What song reminds you most of this OC?

17. Which animal represents them best?

18. If they had a signature scent, what would it be?

19. If they were turned into a doll or figurine, what accessories would be included?

20. Oh no! Your OC magically turned into a dish. What kind of food did they turn into?

21. What would their social media account look like?

22. Which fictional character from an existing IP would they absolutely hate?

23. Why do you love this OC?

## PART IV:LET'S DIG DEEPER

1. Pick a few of your favorite OCs. What do they all have in common? What do you think that reveals about you?

2. Which OC is most misunderstood by your friends & followers? Why do you think others interpret them differently than you intended?

3. If someone analyzed your OC collection without knowing anything about you, what conclusions would they reach about your personality, values, fears, and interests? Would they be accurate?

4. Which OC embodies a version of yourself that you left behind? Which OC embodies the ideal version of you?

5. What recurring wound appears throughout your cast? Abandonment, loneliness, duty, failure, etc.? Why do you think that wound appears so often?

6. Pick an OC story or universe of yours. What question is this story trying to answer?

7. Which aspect of yourself appears in your villains but rarely in your protagonists?

8. Which OC would understand your darkest thought without judging you? Which OC contains the impulses you refuse to act upon>

9. What would your oldest OC say about the person you've become?

## PART V: FINAL QUESTION:

1. Years from now when you are old and grey, long after you've forgotten details, abandoned projects, and your desk is littered with unfinished drafts…one OC remains beside you. Who is it? Why were they the one that stayed?
